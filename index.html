The FizzBuzz App

"Fizzbuzz" the coding challenge, was originated by Imran Ghoryin in the year of 2007. Since the creation of the code, the Fizzbuzz challenge has been used by many interviewers as a tool to test the knowledge of a Web Developer . Allows a user to enter two integers and print the appropriate result based on multiples of the users input; replace any number divisible by 3 with "fizz" and any number divisible by 5 with "buzz".
